Serving the communities of Puyallup, Graham, Eatonville, Bonney Lake, Milton, Edgewood, Auburn, Federal Way, Kent, and Enumclaw, private lessons are conducted at your home, or a park local to you. Private sessions are $80 per session, with most sessions being approximately an hour long. Weekend and weekday availability - contact for further information, contact information below.

Limited group lessons are offered at Bradley Lake Park in Puyallup. At least one private session is required first, so that group classes can be structured to benefit all dogs and people participating. Group sessions can consist of group obedience work and pack walks, with limited dog play a possiblity depending on personality and skill level of dogs involved.
